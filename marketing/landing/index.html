FAQ

Good questions.

Do I need an account to start?

No. You can onboard and start practicing right away as a guest; your progress is saved on your device. Create an account when you want to sync to another phone.

I'm not moving for a tech job — is this for me?

Yes. The focus is IT relocation, but the engine is domain-parametric: tell it your field and goals and the examples follow. The personalization happens under the hood.

What level do I need?

A short placement at the start finds your level, then the app adapts the difficulty to you as you go.

Are explanations in my language?

When you miss one, the explanation is given in your language so the grammar point actually lands.

Is it free? Any ads?

You can train for free, and there are no ads. An optional paid tier — "Black Belt" (Чёрный пояс) — lifts the daily cap and unlocks extras, but it's never required to keep training.
